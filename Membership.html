Our Process

First Contact

If you are interested in Your Digs fill out the enquiry form and someone from the New Membership Team will give you a call. In this phone call we discuss a little more about Your Digs and what it is we do and also get some basic information from you. 

Chat

This is a stage that everyone who is interested in a Your Digs house has to go through. We'll get to know you  and what it is about Your Digs that you like/dislike. We find out more about you as a person and assess whether you would fit with our community. If you're not currently based local to our offices we can do an interview over FaceTime or Skype

Viewings

If after the interview we are happy to proceed with the application we will book in viewings. It is important for you to make the right decision and giving you a feel for what else is out there is always going to help with this.

Move In!

You've filled out all your information, paid and your membership has been approved. Next step is to select a move in date. This is where you'll meet your Member Services Executive and collect your keys 

Meet Housemates!

You’re moving in but you're yet to meet any of your housemates. It feels kind of weird. When we book your check in you'll be included in an email introducing you to your housemates. This is a good time to reply to all and say hi! All houses have house Whatsapp groups so get involved in and arrange a meal or drinks with your housemates as soon as possible after you move in so you can get to know each other. It's also a good idea to find out how the house currently works in terms of a bin rota and anything they do as a house that's worth you knowing.
